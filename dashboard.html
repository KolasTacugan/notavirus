Happy 2nd Monthsary, my wife!

Happy 2nd Monthsary, baby.

Sorry this is a little late — but like I said, late is better than absent. And honestly, I didn’t want to write something just to be on time without actually putting my heart into it. You deserve something real, not rushed.

You’ve been so understanding of me. Truly. I know things have been tough lately — or maybe I’ve been tough. I haven’t always been kind, and I haven’t always felt like myself. Thank you for staying. For choosing us even on the days I feel lost, anxious, or hard to love. That means more to me than you know.

You’ve been the best partner, and sometimes I genuinely don’t know what I did to deserve you. I may not always express it well or show it the way you need, but please know this — I’m always excited to see you. It’s always you I look forward to. Even when I’m busy. Even when I seem distant or unreachable. It’s still you.

You will always be the love of my life. I love you.

— Kolas
