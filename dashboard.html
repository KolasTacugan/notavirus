Happy 3rd Monthsary, my wife!

Happy 3rd Monthsary, baby.

my baby, happy monthsary.

first, i want to apologize if this letter is a bit late. i didn’t want to write something just because i had to. today, i finally had the energy and the right emotions to sit down and write this for you.

when i said i couldn’t love you the way i want to, most of what i meant was my pride. and i know how much that has caused some of our fights. as much as i say i’m content with our ldr situation, let’s be honest—it does get hard sometimes. there are moments where i know i could’ve handled things better if you were here.

during our off calls, i’ve felt a lot of guilt about how i talk to you or how i act. i keep thinking things would’ve been different if you were here. that’s why i always say babawi ako when andito ka na. but it hit me—you’re still not here, and this is our reality. i need to learn how to be better for you within the situation we have now.

for all the dates i can’t take you on yet, and all the ways i want to treat you, i realized that what matters more right now is how i show up for you, especially when we don’t agree. i’m sorry if you’ve had to deal with parts of me that are still healing.

i’m starting to accept that i am an emotional person, no matter how much my pride and ego try to deny it. and if i want to love you better, i have to face that.

thank you for being patient with me. even from a distance, you find ways to calm the parts of me that feel chaotic. i don’t even know how you do that without being here, without holding me—but you do.

i love you, baby. i love us. even if we’re miles apart, there’s no one else i’d rather go through this with. you’ve been an amazing partner.

happy monthsary.
